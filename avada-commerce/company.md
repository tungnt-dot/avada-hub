# Company Overview

- **Name:** Avada Commerce (thuộc Avada Group)
- **Website:** https://avada.io
- **Industry:** Ecommerce SaaS
- **Headquarters:** Ha Noi, Vietnam
- **Founded:** ~2015
- **Ecosystem:** Shopify apps

## Mission
Help ecommerce businesses increase traffic, conversion, retention, and ROI by optimizing the entire buyer journey from first visit to repeat purchase.

## Strategic Advantage
1. **Full ecosystem** — Solutions for the entire customer journey, not just one tool
2. **Modular apps** — Merchants install only what they need

## Content Strategy
Avada runs an ecommerce blog, Shopify tutorials, and app comparison guides to help merchants learn, choose tools, and optimize their store. Strong topical authority SEO potential from wide product ecosystem and clear audience.

## Reputation
Highly rated for ease of use, feature richness, responsive support, and flexible customization.
